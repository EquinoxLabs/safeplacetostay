‘Safe Place to Stay’ is a certification program that will ensure that the areas of physical security, fire safety, food safety, environment safety, and overall occupant safety meet the required standards.

Why Safe Place to Stay?

Accommodation establishments host guests from different backgrounds in close cohabitation. Making such places extremely susceptible to various contagions and dangerous contaminants.
Contemporary consumers realizing these dangers, look for safety in accommodation services and demand high standards of hygiene and safety be followed by facilities.
The existing standards followed by the sector, however, do not include many measures that are of importance in today's world. This is why the introduction of newer streamlined standards that ensure complete health safety & security of guests, is the need of the hour.
