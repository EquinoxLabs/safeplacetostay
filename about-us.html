‘Safe Place to Stay’ is an initiative that aims to update the guest safety parameters followed by the accommodation sector according to the needs of the contemporary world. It audits and certifies the tremendous amount of time, money and effort, hospitality organizations take in order to provide their guests with a safe & secure experience.
Under this program, reluctant organizations of the accommodation sector are audited based on a set of distinctive standards curated by industry experts. The organizations, as per the level of their compliance are then awarded & attested with a ‘Safe Place to Stay’ certificate that is recognized nationwide.
