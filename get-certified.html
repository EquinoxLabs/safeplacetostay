Who Needs the ‘Safe Place to Stay’ Certification?

Any accommodation providing organization that

  • hosts guests from various different locations
  • guests spend long hours within the premises interacting with the facility
  • guests depend on the accommodation premises for food, water & other basic necessities
  • management staff interacts and takes care of guest needs with direct interaction
  • wants to ensure their guests’ and staff’s well-being & health security
  • wishes to get their efforts toward guest health, safety & security recognized

needs to get a ‘Safe Place to Stay’ certification.

Based on the accommodation industry’s requirements, our experts have curated a set of specific parameters that help test and establish health and safety measures in hotels, motels, resorts and other similar organizations. Here is an overview of some of the Safe Place to Stay parameters that’ll help firms understand and establish themselves as a Safe Place to Accommodate in India.

How to Get Certified

You can become a ‘Safe Place to Stay’ by following these 4-steps leading to a safety standards audit and receive a Safe Place to Stay Certification.

Registering under the program

The step includes enrolling your Facility under the ‘Safe Place to Stay’ Initiative by filling the form in our registration section.

Stage 1 Audit

The stage 1 audit will help you understand your level of compliance with the standard.

Stage 2 Audit (Final Audit)

This will be the final assessment of your adherence to the standard. If you score 70% and above you will be certified as “Safe Place to Stay.”

Issuing of Certificate

If you qualify as Safe Place to Stay, you will be issued a certificate and usage guidelines.
